FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our roofing services.

How long does a roof replacement take? +
Most residential roof replacements take 1-3 days depending on the size and complexity. We work efficiently while maintaining the highest quality standards. Commercial projects may take longer depending on the scope.
Do you offer warranties? +
Yes! We offer comprehensive warranties on both materials and workmanship. Material warranties range from 25 years to lifetime depending on the product. Our workmanship is backed by a 10-year labor warranty.
How much does a new roof cost? +
Roof costs vary based on size, materials, and complexity. A typical residential roof in Texas ranges from $8,000-$25,000. We provide free detailed estimates with transparent pricing and no hidden fees.
Can you work with my insurance company? +
Absolutely! We have extensive experience working with all major insurance companies. We'll handle the entire claims process, including documentation, estimates, and negotiations to ensure you get the coverage you deserve.
Are you licensed and insured? +
Yes, we are fully licensed, bonded, and insured in Texas. Our license number is #123456. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation to protect you and our team.
Do you offer emergency roofing services? +
Yes! We provide 24/7 emergency roofing services throughout Texas. Whether it's storm damage, leaks, or structural issues, our rapid response team can be on-site within 30 minutes in most cases.
